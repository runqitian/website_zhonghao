Supplier and Buyer Identification

Zhonghao International LLC will initially identify potential clients, their requirements, and their target market. According to the descriptions and details, our company will find potential upstream suppliers and customize the products. After initial consultations with clients, the Company will contact suppliers and negotiate procurement of the requested products.

The Company mainly provide brokerage services for a selection of electrical equipment procured in global market. The electrical equipment will meet a wide range of specifications, large and small, powered and unpowered, and will include liquid oxygen pumps, pressure transmitters, high-pressure vessels, high temperature preheater feed pumps, and many others. Diverse industries use electrical equipment in their business activities, mainly in production processes. The Company will approach companies in the construction and manufacturing industries as well as wholesalers and retailers and assess their requirements. Zhonghao International LLC will customize its product offerings according to the client’s preferences and current market trends.

Contracting & Consulting

As an international trade agent, Zhonghao International LLC will act as intermediary among electrical equipment and other manufacturers and end-users, and charge a commission fee per purchase order. After negotiations with our clients, once an agreement on product specification, amount, and price is reached, we will start to prepare legal documents. As an international trade agent, Zhonghao International LLC will coordinate credit and financial activities and obtain payments for import and export operations. The Company will act as an advisor on matters of tariffs, markets, and federal and foreign regulations. Furthermore, Zhonghao International LLC will ensure that all filings, documents, and regulatory reporting are completed accurately, in a timely manner, and according to trade laws. The Company’s team will keep informed of international events and changes to laws and requirements that may impact operations.

Shipping Set up / Warehousing Arrangement / Delivery

Our Company will be responsible for organizing shipping of the products. Zhonghao International LLC will offer the complete set of services including placing purchase orders, securing necessary paperwork, warehousing, and transportation. In order to focus on core business activities and reduce expenses, the Company will outsource warehousing and delivery-related activities.

Customer Services

This approach will entail careful and consistent follow-up to ensure that retailers, wholesalers, and end-users receive goods on time. Zhonghao International LLC will strive to obtain constant feedback from customers in order to upgrade its Zhonghao International LLC offering. This strategy will allow the Company to build brand awareness and customer loyalty, therefore increasing sales.
